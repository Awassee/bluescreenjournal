# v2.2.2 Patch Plan

This patch line follows the `v2.2.1` release.

The goal is to keep pushing on trust, safety, and long-session usability without turning the app into a cluttered control panel.

## Candidate patch work

1. Improve conflict merge ergonomics.
   - Acceptance:
   - conflicted dates are easier to understand before entering merge
   - the merge screen makes source A, source B, and the merged result feel safer to compare

2. Strengthen search as a review tool.
   - Acceptance:
   - search presets and pinned searches feel more intentional
   - recurring review flows are easier to reach from menus

3. Add safer restore and recovery drills in-product.
   - Acceptance:
   - backup and cloud recovery paths feel rehearsed, not theoretical
   - users can preview what recovery will do before committing

4. Improve cloud sync observability.
   - Acceptance:
   - active backend, target, connector readiness, and last successful sync are obvious at a glance

5. Deepen spellcheck into a better writing assistant.
   - Acceptance:
   - session dictionary and personal-dictionary workflow are easier to understand
   - typo fixes remain menu-first and low-friction
